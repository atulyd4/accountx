Accountax features

Getting Started

# Signup

Fill all details and click on signup button.

# Onboarding

When you login first time you above form where you get can chose your currency symbol
and timezone.it is one time process.

# Adding account

click on Add account
This feature will allow the app users to include the customer details and it is a one-time task.
This is similar to making an entry of each customer on an individual page of a manual accounting book.

# Create Entry

click on create entry
In this feature you include all transactions that happen with other persons.
you can explain the transaction with the amount paid, or received with purpose.
Now that you have added the Accounts you can select on the name to add the transactions

# Show

click on show
In this feature you can see full detail of entry amount paid, or received with purpose.

# Transactions

when you click on transactions
you find all transactions related to that account.

# Export csv file

click on Export csv
you can download your entries in csv file
if search is applied then you find search entries as csv othervise you find total entries.

# Dashboard

On dashboard you will see total amount you paid(debit)/recived(credit),abilable balance
and latest five entries at present time.

# update profile & change password

click on profile
you can change any details related to your profile.
click on change password botton to change your password.

# Delete account

click on profile
Just in case you want to close the accountx to clear your transactions and start a new one,
then you can quickly click on the Delete option and close your account. It is always possible to create or delete an account any time you wish to.
